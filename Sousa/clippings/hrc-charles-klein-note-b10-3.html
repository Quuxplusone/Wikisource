A short note from playwright Charles Klein dated July 22, 1910, apparently requesting his secretary to duplicate a stage adaptation of his novel Maggie Pepper. According to the New York Public Library, this adaptation of Maggie Pepper was (first?) produced at the Harris Theatre, New York, on August 31, 1911.

The autograph original is in the Charles Klein collection (MS-02316) at the Harry Ransom Center (University of Texas–Austin), box 10, folder 3.

Shirley Manor,
Rowayton, Conn. July 22–10

My dear Miss Walsh,

I am sending you today via Adams Ex. script of Maggie Pepper as corrected. Please make three copies as soon as possible, and when you receive the script let me know.

Yours Sincerely,
Chas. Klein
